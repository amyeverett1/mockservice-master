Mars Emigration Visa information

Mars Emigration Visa's are valid indefinately.

The cost of this visa is £103,349.00. You must repay any money you’re asked to. If you don’t repay money when asked you could be taken to court, and any benefits you receive may be reduced or money taken directly from your wages until your debt is repaid.

Space travel is dangerous and you undertake it at your own personal risk.

Your visa will be revoked if you or any named person on your visa is charged with a criminal offence. In these circumstances you will face extradition back to Earth.
